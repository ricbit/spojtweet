SpojTweet

Welcome to SpojTweet!

If you sign up to SpojTweet, then we can automatically send tweets on your behalf, every time you solve a problem on SPOJ, or when you earn a badge.

In order to sign up, you must have a Twitter account. By pressing the button below, you'll be redirected to Twitter, where you must authorize SpojTweet to post tweets for you. Don't worry about flooding your friends, we never send more than one tweet an hour!
